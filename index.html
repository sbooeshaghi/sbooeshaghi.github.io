(118 words) I am a Postdoctoral Scholar at the California Institute of Technology in the Division of Biology and Biological Engineering working with Professor Lior Pachter. I study single-cell genomics technologies and develop computational and hardware tools to produce and analyze sequencing data. I am an engineer and applied mathematician by training and I enjoy biking, hiking, and photography.

I received my PhD in Mechanical Engineering from Caltech in 2022 and I graduated from MIT in 2017 with a B.S. in Mechanical Engineering and a B.S. in Mathematics. While in undergrad I ran Tea with Teachers, MIT-ASME, and xFair - MIT's Spring Career Fair. I also participated in research projects under Professor Evelyn Wang, Professor Baratunde Cola, & Professor Pierre Lermusiaux.
