I study single-cell genomics technologies and develop computational and hardware tools to produce and analyze sequencing data with Professor Lior Pachter. I received my PhD in Mechanical Engineering from Caltech in 2022 and my B.S. in Mechanical Engineering and in Mathematics from MIT in 2017. While in undergrad I ran Tea with Teachers, MIT-ASME, and xFair - MIT's Spring Career Fair. I also participated in research projects under Professor Evelyn Wang, Professor Baratunde Cola, & Professor Pierre Lermusiaux. I am an engineer and applied mathematician by training and I enjoy biking, hiking, and photography.
